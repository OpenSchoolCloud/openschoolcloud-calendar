OSC Calendar is a secure calendar app designed for Dutch education. Sync with your Nextcloud server via CalDAV and keep all your appointments, schedules, and reminders in one place.

Why OSC Calendar?

Privacy First
No Google, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays in the Netherlands, under your control. Fully open source (AGPL v3).

Nextcloud Sync
Connect to your school cloud via CalDAV. Multiple calendars, automatic sync, and offline access.

Quick Event Creation
Just type "tomorrow 2pm meeting" and the event is created automatically. Works in Dutch and English.

Home Screen Widgets
View today's appointments or your next event directly on your home screen.

Reminders
Set reminders so you never miss an appointment.

Booking Links & QR Codes
Share booking links for parent-teacher conferences, including QR codes.

Dark Mode
Easy on your eyes, day and night.

OSC Calendar is developed by OpenSchoolCloud, a Dutch initiative for privacy-friendly school solutions. The app is fully open source and can be audited by anyone.
